Frequently Asked Questions

What is Banger?

Banger is an AI-powered tool that transforms your rough daily build notes into polished, engaging X posts. Built specifically for indie hackers, developers, and creators who build in public.

Will my posts sound like AI wrote them?

No. Banger is specifically designed to preserve your authentic voice. The AI enhances structure and engagement while keeping your unique personality and tone intact.

Is Banger free to use?

Yes. Banger offers a free tier with 3 generations per day. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited generations, Git integration, engagement tracking, and all advanced features. See Pricing.

How is Banger different from ChatGPT?

Generic AI tools produce generic content. Banger is trained specifically on the build-in-public community — it understands shipping culture, technical updates, and the unique voice of indie hackers.

What platforms does Banger support?

Banger is currently optimized for X (Twitter). LinkedIn, Threads, and Bluesky support is coming soon based on user demand.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We don't store your raw notes beyond your session. Your X credentials are handled securely through OAuth. We never have access to your password. See our Privacy Policy.
